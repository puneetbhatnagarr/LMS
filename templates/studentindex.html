From Admissions to Academic Advisement


With so many students seeking various degree requirements, the Student Affairs Offices of many colleges employ a full retinue of advisors for several areas of concern. Whether it pertains to meeting criteria for admission to the university or involves guiding students towards their academic goals, this area of responsibility may appear to be a collegiate entity all its own. According to an essay by Dhanfu E. Elston, These professionals are key to assisting students in their quest to complete college and move on to offer value and services to their world.
